Short CV

I'm a research and development engineer at Coronis Computing S.L.. I did my PhD at the Computer Vision and Robotics Group (VICOROB) of the University of Girona, as a member of the Underwater Vision and Robotics Research Center (CIRS). My research interests are focused on 2D and 3D reconstruction from optical data.

Academic Formation:
  • PhD in Technology (UdG, 2014)
  • Master in Automation, Computation and Systems (UdG, 2010)
  • Information Systems Engineer (UdG, 2009)
  • Computer Science Engineer (UdG, 2007)
